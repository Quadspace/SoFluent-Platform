# So Fluent Branding Integration Guide

**Brand Assets Location:**
- Brand Manual: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/108UeI-Mr564Q05WOUT8SMKyFvARBj69q
- Headshots: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1al6GyDwIFla5fnQ7z5BcNIcX-tPu8DV7

## Brand Assets Structure

### From Brand Manual Folder:
1. **Manual de Marca** - Brand guidelines
2. **Logo** - So Fluent logo files
3. **Padrões e Grafismos** - Patterns and graphics
4. **Fontes** - Brand fonts
5. **Cartão de Visita** - Business card templates

### From Headshots Folder:
- 50+ professional headshot images (IMG_0005.jpg through IMG_9846.jpg)
- High-resolution photos for testimonials, team, and about sections

## Implementation Steps

1. Download brand assets from Google Drive
2. Place in `client/src/assets/branding/` folder
3. Update components to use brand fonts, colors, and logos
4. Integrate headshots into testimonials and about sections
5. Apply brand patterns and graphics throughout
