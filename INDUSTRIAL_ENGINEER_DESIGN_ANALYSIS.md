### 1. **Hero Section**
- **Massive Typography:** 80-100px headings
- **Split Layout:** Content left, visual right (or centered with visual)
- **Floating Elements:** Stat cards, badges, CTAs
- **Gradient Overlays:** Subtle, professional
- **Video/Image Showcase:** Large, prominent

### 2. **Navigation**
- **Fixed/Sticky:** Always visible
- **Backdrop Blur:** Modern glass effect
- **Minimal Design:** Clean, focused
- **Smooth Transitions:** Hover effects

### 3. **Content Sections**
- **Card Grids:** Clean, organized
- **Alternating Backgrounds:** Visual rhythm
- **Generous Spacing:** Breathing room
- **Icon Integration:** Visual hierarchy

### 4. **Typography Scale**
- **H1:** 72-96px (hero)
- **H2:** 48-64px (sections)
- **H3:** 32-40px (subsections)
- **Body:** 16-18px (readable)

### 5. **Visual Elements**
- **Gradients:** Subtle, professional
- **Shadows:** Depth and elevation
- **Animations:** Smooth, purposeful
- **Whitespace:** Strategic spacing
